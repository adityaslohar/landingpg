General

MedGlobe.com is a comprehensive medical tourism platform that connects patients with top-quality healthcare providers worldwide. We facilitate seamless access to medical treatments abroad by providing detailed information about hospitals, doctors, procedures, and travel arrangements, ensuring you receive the best possible care at competitive prices.

Medical tourism involves traveling to another country for medical care. People choose this option for various reasons including cost savings (treatments can be 60-80% cheaper), shorter waiting times, access to advanced procedures not available locally, combining treatment with vacation, and receiving care from world-renowned specialists and state-of-the-art facilities.

Visa requirements vary by destination country and your nationality. Most medical tourism destinations offer medical visas or tourist visas suitable for treatment purposes. Common requirements include a valid passport, medical documents from the treating hospital, proof of financial means, and sometimes a letter from your home country doctor. We provide detailed visa guidance for each destination and can assist with the application process.

Our basic consultation and information services are completely free. We earn through partnerships with healthcare providers, so patients don't pay extra fees. However, some premium services like dedicated case management, travel coordination, or expedited processing may have associated fees, which are clearly communicated upfront. There are no hidden charges, and we maintain full transparency about all costs involved.

We thoroughly vet all healthcare providers in our network. You can verify credentials through international accreditations (JCI, ISO), medical board certifications, doctor qualifications and experience, patient reviews and testimonials, hospital rankings and awards. We provide detailed profiles including success rates, specializations, and facility certifications. Additionally, we can arrange virtual consultations to help you make informed decisions.
